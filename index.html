Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh is now developing frontier technologies such as artificial intelligence, robotics, blockchain, Internet of Things (IoT), and preparing for the challenges of the Fourth Industrial Revolution (4IR).

Sustainability

The idea of the Digital Bangladesh vision was to promote harmonised and sustainable digitisation so that society as a whole can benefit from digitisation.

Infrastructure

Bangladesh’s “phygital public infrastructure” bridges DPI theory and practice.Unlike physical infrastructure, which has limited construction methods, DPI presents virtually limitless possibilities.

Empowerment

Bangladesh has made remarkable progress in reducing poverty, supported by sustained economic growth.By the blessings of almighty the state have ensured the empowerment of people from all walk of life.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

“The revolution that we are witnessing is not just about changing political systems; it is about redefining the very essence of Bangladesh. It is about empowering our youth, nurturing innovation, and embracing a future where social change is at the heart of every decision. Bangladesh 2.0 is not a mere continuation—it is a bold transformation, driven by the resilience of our people and the collective will to build a society that thrives on equality, opportunity, and sustainability. The true power of this revolution lies not in the hands of a few, but in the dreams and actions of each individual, from the streets to the boardrooms. This is the dawn of a new era where social impact, innovation, and compassion lead the way forward.”

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
